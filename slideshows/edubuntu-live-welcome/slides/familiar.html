Make yourself home

Edubuntu makes use of many design elements popular in other operating systems and even some improvements. We're confident that whatever your computing background is, you'll feel at home with Edubuntu in no time at all.

Where to find things:

Launch applications via the Applications menu on the top of your display.
Close windows by clicking the red X in the top left corner.
Shut down or reboot by clicking the power button on the top right of your screen.
